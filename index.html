Hello! I’m Kameron Guajardo from Texas.

I am the 3rd child out of the 6 children from my loving family! Ever since I was young, I played video games for a LONG time. I’m talking since I was 3 or 4, so I’ve been in love with video games for nearly my whole life. I loved some old dead MOBAS and still like playing them and I adore platform fighters. Those are my main games, with the exception of hero shooters from time to time if I like them. Single player games are another story you can ask me about… Anyways, my music journey started at the beginning of 7th grade (2016). I started my years playing the trombone until I finished high school. In 2023, I started writing music. That same year, I made my first ever video game ost for my friend, and ever since I have been making music for video games! I have a lasting appreciation and love for music and video games! I hope you guys enjoy the music and stay cool
